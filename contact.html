What we welcome

  • Story tips — research, case studies, or practices we should cover
  • Research you've found compelling — particularly peer-reviewed work on somatic therapy, polyvagal theory, IFS, or grief science
  • Guest pitches — we occasionally publish guest pieces from somatic practitioners, grief counselors, and researchers. Pitches should include a brief bio and a 2-3 sentence summary of the piece you'd like to write.
  • Corrections — if you find a factual error in an article, please tell us. We take accuracy seriously and correct errors promptly.

What we don't accept

  • Sponsored content or product placements
  • Guest posts that are primarily promotional
  • Link exchange requests

We try to respond to all emails within 5 business days. We are a small editorial team and cannot guarantee responses to every pitch.

We are not a mental health service. We cannot provide personal guidance, referrals, or crisis support. If you are in crisis, please contact a licensed mental health professional or call a crisis line in your country.
