The practices, rituals, and frameworks described on this site — including those drawing on polyvagal theory, IFS (Internal Family Systems), and somatic therapy — are educational in nature. They are not clinical interventions.

If you are experiencing acute grief, depression, post-traumatic stress, suicidal ideation, or any other mental health crisis, please seek support from a licensed mental health professional. Do not use content from this site as a replacement for that care.

Nothing on this site constitutes medical advice. The Ritual Journal Collective does not diagnose, treat, or claim to treat any medical or psychological condition. Any descriptions of physical or emotional experiences in our articles reflect the observations and experiences of our authors — not clinical findings applied to your situation.

Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making decisions about your mental or physical health.
